An Introduction to Me:


While working through an illness in my teenage years, I worked hard to keep my grades up so I could eventually get into college, which I did, after graduating with a high GPA. Then, I did the same in community college, graduating summa cum laude as a member of the honors societies "Phi Theta Kappa" and "The National Collegiate Chinese Honors Society." Since then, I have gone into remission, I have recently earned an academic scholarship within two semesters after transferring to George Mason University, and I have maintained my 4.0 GPA.

All of these accomplishments were certainly not easy to work toward, and because of this, they are perfect examples of my work ethic as well as my desire to truly learn the information being taught to me and do well rather than memorize the answers and eventually forget them soon after the class is over. This certainly will be a quality I carry into my career.

However, academics is not the only area to which I have dedicated my time. In my free time, I love to read manga and webcomics, draw, and play co-op games with friends. Recently, I have taken steps toward creating my own webcomics one day by enrolling in drawing basics courses in order to improve the scientific side of my drawing skills. Additionally, I have given time to my decade-old pet care business, combining my love for animals with gaining experience in running a business (such as maintaining good communication skills, budgeting time, and anticipating clients' needs, which includes surprise transportation delays and supply shortages for feeding the pets).
