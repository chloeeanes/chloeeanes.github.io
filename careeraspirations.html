My Career Goals

As I work toward my degree in English with a concentration in Writing and Rhetoric, I aim to enter the field of print production and/or web design. My dream since I was little was to become an author. However, I realize that there is much to learn about the field of publishing. Therefore, I am very interested to see what it is like from the other side of things and learning what work goes into putting new books on shelves will tell me all I need to know. On the web design side of my aspirations, this interest of mine links back to my gaming days, when I took a shot at coding inside them, such as learning and memorizing the right commands in Minecraft. Later on, I noticed how many small businesses present themselves through their websites and thought to myself "wouldn't it be cool if I could make one of these from scratch," leading to my interest in website coding. Even though I understand that taking on a job in both fields at once would be overwhelming, one day, I hope to dip my toes in both worlds, so I have made brief short-term goals and long-term goals lists including both careers to reflect my aspirations.

Short-Term Goals

  • Receive training in coding for the web
  • Research nearest publishing houses
  • Learn the ins-and-outs of print production

Long-Term Goals

  • Secure internships/temp positions in either field
  • Secure a full-time position as a production editor or a web designer
  • When needed, update knowledge on coding languages (this applies to technology used in publishing houses as well)
